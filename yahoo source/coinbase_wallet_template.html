Secure Your Coinbase Account

At Coinbase, we take your security seriously. We're constantly updating our security measures to ensure your assets remain safe and protected.

Why Choose a Self-Custody Wallet?

A self-custody wallet, such as Coinbase Wallet, offers you full control over your assets and private keys, minimizing reliance on third-party storage. You are the sole owner of your funds.

How to Activate Your Coinbase Wallet

Follow these steps to set up your Coinbase Wallet securely:

  1. Download the Coinbase Wallet app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. Securely store your recovery phrase. This phrase is critical for wallet access and should never be shared with anyone, including Coinbase.
  3. During setup, select 'I already have a wallet' and input the the recovery phrase in the box.
  4. Once setup is complete, transfer your assets to the Coinbase Wallet for regain control over your digital funds.

Security Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all of your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Ensure your devices and apps are always up to date.
  • Stay alert to phishing scams and suspicious communications.
